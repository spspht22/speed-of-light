Our story

Simply for the love of music, our band has come together with different backgrounds of musical talents to form a unique sound that all generations will appreciate. We are located in Novato CA where we perform on a weekly basis at Hopkins restaurant, our favorite home town spot where music expression is welcomed. Recently, we have thrown concerts to raise funds for the victims that were affected by the Northern CA wildfires.
